Culinary courses



Cook with us

This intensive two-month course in modern cooking and confectionery is designed to provide participants with a solid foundation and advanced techniques for ambitious chefs. The small amount of teaching required allows our experienced professors-cooks to give students a lot of attention to individual students.

Vegetarian

In this course, participants spend 80% of their time in class gaining practical experience in the kitchen. The course includes vegetarian and vegan products, sweet and savory treats, as well as basic Italian and French cuisine.

That something is sweet to me

A recipe is never just a recipe and that is why you always browse cookbooks over and over again and read them over and over again. There is always a new charm and satisfaction in every ingredient. Explore the new beauties of sweet cuisine and peek into the world of sweets and delicious desserts.

Bread as a soul

Enjoying a warm pastry in the early morning hours while the sun gradually illuminates the rays of the corner of the window is a sight we have all experienced and to which we gladly return every time. The preparation of various pastries and breads creates an even better feeling and a better pleasure. The "Bread as a Soul" blog is the right place where you can find a way to prepare the most beautiful pastries through a multitude of innovative techniques and through the use of modern tools used in French bakeries.
